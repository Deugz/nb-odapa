# About ODaPA 2022  

<p class="emphase2"> â€œOpening the Dialogueâ€</p>

***

The first *Open Dialogues Across Physics and Astronomy* ran at the start of September 2022. It aimed to be a truly cross-discipline conference, welcoming PhD students from all physics and astronomy disciplines to learn from academics outside of their own silos. It was run by IPPSnet, SEPnetâ€™s Intersectional PhD Peer Support network with the goal creating a conference environment that was welcoming to PhD students from all backgrounds.

In total, 17 students and 8 invited academics attended and presented an amazing array of talks from fields including: astrophysics, condensed matter physics, biophysics, material science, astrochemistry, quantum technology, planetary geoscience, particle physics and physics education research. It was a resounding success!
